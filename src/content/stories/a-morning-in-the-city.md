The alarm rang at seven o'clock, and Maya opened her eyes slowly.

Outside her window, the city was already awake. Cars moved along the street, and birds sang on the rooftops.

Maya got out of bed and opened the curtains. The morning sun filled her small apartment with soft light.

She made a cup of coffee and some warm toast. The kitchen smelled wonderful. She ate by the window and watched the people below.

After breakfast, she got dressed and put on her favorite blue jacket. She picked up her bag and her keys.

Maya walked to the bus stop on the corner. The air was cool and fresh. A neighbor smiled and said good morning.

The bus arrived, and Maya found a seat by the window. She liked to watch the city while she traveled to work.

The streets were busy. People were walking quickly, and shops were opening their doors. A man was selling flowers on the sidewalk.

Maya got off near a small park. She had ten minutes, so she sat on a bench under a tree.

A little dog ran past her, and its owner laughed. The morning felt calm and happy.

Then Maya stood up and walked to her office. She felt ready for the day.

She loved her quiet mornings in the busy city. They were simple, but they made her smile.
