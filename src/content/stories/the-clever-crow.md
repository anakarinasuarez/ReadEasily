A crow is very thirsty. She wants water. The sun is hot. She flies and flies.

She finds a tall jug. There is water inside. The crow is happy. She puts her beak in.

But the water is low. Her beak cannot reach it. She tries again. She still cannot drink.

The crow thinks. She looks around. She sees small stones on the ground.

She has an idea. She picks up a stone. She drops it in the jug. Plop!

She drops more stones. One, two, three. The water goes up. It comes a little higher.

The crow drops many stones. The water rises to the top. Now she can reach it.

The crow drinks the cool water. She is not thirsty now. She feels glad and proud.

A good idea is better than strong wings.
