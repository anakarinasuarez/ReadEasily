title: The Tortoise and the Hare
level: A1
category: fables

The hare runs very fast. He is proud. He laughs at the slow tortoise.

One day the hare says, "Let's race!" The tortoise says yes. The animals watch.

They start to run. The hare runs ahead. He looks back. The tortoise is far behind.

"I am too fast," the hare says. "I can rest." He lies down. Soon he sleeps.

The tortoise walks and walks. He is slow but he does not stop. Step by step, he passes the hare.

The hare wakes up. He sees the finish line. The tortoise is almost there! The hare runs fast. But he is too late.

The tortoise wins the race. All the animals cheer. The hare is sad and quiet.

Slow and steady can win the race.
